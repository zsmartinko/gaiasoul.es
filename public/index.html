Hello Dear Woman,

I'm Sofia, the creator of Gaia Soul.

Mom of two, pregnancy massage therapist, ceremony holder, prenatal & birth doula in Marbella and Estepona. I was a girl who loved to feel the ground beneath her feet. Wondering about the different textures of grass, the darkness of freshly laid asphalt, the pebbles shaped over time. A child without a sense of time where only thoughts exist. Then a young woman, sitting in a desert, with people around her, and there was it again, that same timeless mindset. The silence deepened and took on a sound, waved and pulsed, just like our hearts, our blood. Our births. And as a woman I gave birth. It felt like a storm sweeping through my body, taking every thought and reason. Birth takes over with its elemental nature and in this time of deep transformation I created this space to offer gentle support through touch, ritual and presence. I am here to walk with you in trust, love and care.

Breastfeeding Support

Why prepare for breastfeeding when it's in our nature? Why seek help and support? How can something so natural feel so overwhelming?

Ancient women were never alone. They never had to face motherhood alone. By the time they had their babies, they had already witnessed their mothers, aunts and sisters breastfeed, seen the challanges that could arise, experienced all the care and held those babies in their arms. This wisdom, this silent and organic knowledge passed down through generations, is what we are often missing and where doula care and breastfeeding support truly make sense.

Massage Ritual

When we touch, we comunicate. Our essence is revealed. A touch tells stories of who we are, where we come from, and where we're headed. Stories of joy and sorrow. Of wounds and healing. Of pasts we carry and longings we pursue. A touch creates space for trust, for safety, for understanding, for healing. A massage relieves the body and calms the mind, a ritual embraces the soul.
Ceremonies

A Mother Blessing Ceremony is a rite of passage that guides the body, mind and soul through this transitional stage of human life. Gives time and space for acceptance of change, of letting go the old and embracing a new self. This soulful gathering offers an intimate and profound way to feel connected to all the women who have given birth before you and those who will give birth after you.
